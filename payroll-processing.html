Accounts Payroll Process

Payroll accounting is integral to every business, no matter the size. We provide accounting & bookkeeping management services to customers all over the globe, including customers in the UK and USA, key steps of which are -

  • Payroll data processing and tax calculation
  • Calculating the net salary payable to the employees after deductions
  • Paystubs and paychecks generation
  • Check processing and delivery and direct deposits
  • Payroll reporting and filing of W2 and W4
  • 940s and 941s
  • Payroll tax returns, and quarterly and annual tax reports
  • Employee Insurance, pension, and 401K Retirement Plan.
